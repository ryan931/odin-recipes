Chicken Parm

Chef Inspired Chicken Parm

This chef inspired chicken parm will reall impress. Made with fresh mozzarella and and tangy marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 large skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ¾ cup dry bread crumbs
  • ½ cup light olive oil for frying, or as needed
  • Jar of marinara
